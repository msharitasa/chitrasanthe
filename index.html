Introduction

Chitra Sante - "ART FOR ALL" is the 17th successive annual event/ fiar being organised by Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath, Bengaluru, on Sunday, 5th of January 2020.



This event will spread out from Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath on to the Kumara Krupa Road, Bengaluru, at designated areas. This one-day mega event will showcase artists from all over the country. It is a unique opportunity to showcase art to the public. The culture event will display affordable art like Drawings, Paintings, Graphic Prints, Mainature Formats, Sculpure and much more. This is an attempt to bring art to the public, to make art a part of everday life. It also introdces affordable visual art to all.
