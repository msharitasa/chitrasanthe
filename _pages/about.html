About

Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath is a unique multi-dimensional Art Complex, which meets international standards of visual art galleries, attracts artists and institutions from all over the world.

Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath has a comprehensive collection of Leather Puppets, Traditional, Folk, Tribal and Contemporary Art, Sculpture, etc.

Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath is an ideal location in the Central Business District area of Bengaluru for art and art activities and it is the most sought after visual art exhibition venue in the city, attracting buyers, art collectors and connoisseurs. This is the best platform for artists and art lovers.

The College of Fine Arts, a wing of Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath, is a visual arts college that offers Bachelors and Masters' Degree courses (Painting, Sculpture, Graphic, Applied Art and Art History). Animation is an added feature to the existing courses. There s a Ph.D. research centre also in the college.
